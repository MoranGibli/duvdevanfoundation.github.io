2019

Annual Report 2019

click here
During the past year, the Foundation has been preparing a PTSD program, specialized for the Unit's graduates. The treatment procedure is set to begin in the start of the next year, and to be implemented with the full cooperation of the unit. Our aim is to diagnose and assist serving combat soldiers and graduates who have been affected by their combat experience, with collaboration of the NATAL organization and the Scheinberg Fund.

About the Foundation

The Duvdevan Foundation was established in 1992 by a bereaved father who lost his son in the Duvdevan Unit. This Foundation has been recognized as a legal charity by federal law and is registered as “Friends of Duvdevan" (Tax ID – 83-0860160
