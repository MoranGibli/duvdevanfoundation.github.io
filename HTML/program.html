About the Foundation and Programs

The Duvdevan Foundation was established in 1992 by a bereaved father who lost his son in the Duvdevan Unit. This Foundation has been recognized as a legal charity by federal law and is registered as “Friends of Duvdevan" (Tax ID – 83-0860160). We are here to directly impact the lives of soldiers & veterans of Duvdevan and help them In their transition from intense military service to civilian life. We have taken it upon ourselves to give back to these brave young men and women who chose to risk their lives for the State of Israel and Its people. The Duvdevan Foundation is dedicated to the Zionist values of honesty, comradeship, and social involvement. The "Compounded Impact" – Our agenda is to empower Israeli society by empowering our graduates. This unique circle of giving allows our supporters to participate in making a direct impact on Society itself.

The Foundation’s activities:

Education – Every year out of 120 supported scholarship recipients, we offer 40 new ones. Leadership& Empowerment – A Unique Mentoring program. Professional Career Guidance. Support for post-trauma victims, Support for Corona victims.

Commemoration

Financial and Emotional Support to our bereaved families as well as our lone soldiers. Distribution of food baskets and ongoing assistance throughout the year.
